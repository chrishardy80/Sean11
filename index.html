Sean Conway

Sean started out writing and directing short films and music videos, before getting his first television gig on Shameless.

He then wrote the entire mini-series Hit & Miss starring Chloe Sevigny, before moving to Los Angeles, where he worked for four seasons as writer and executive producer on Ray Donovan starring Liev Schreiber.

He also wrote the Bangkok-set film All I See Is You starring Blake Lively. He is currently developing various projects for both film and television in the UK and in the US, with the following people/ companies; Paul Abbott, Marc Forster, Jonas Akerlund, Eddie Marsan, Warp Films,Carnival Productions, Sid Gentle Films, Chapter One Productions, Red Productions, Fremantle North America. He is represented by Casarotto Ramsay and William Morris Endeavor.
