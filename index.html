Sean Conway
Writer
Director
Producer

Sean started out writing and directing short films and music videos, before getting his first television gig on Shameless.

He then wrote the limited series Hit & Miss starring Chloe Sevigny, before moving to Los Angeles, where he worked for four seasons as writer and executive producer on Ray Donovan starring Liev Schreiber. He also wrote the Bangkok-set film All I See Is You starring Blake Lively.

He is currently developing various projects for both film and television in the UK, the US and Australia, with the following; Paul Abbott, Marc Forster, Jonas Akerlund, Eddie Marsan, Irvine Welsh, Warp, Red, Carnival, Sid Gentle Films, Chapter One, Company Pictures, Fremantle and Playmaker Media.

He is represented by Casarotto Ramsay and William Morris Endeavor.
